# SmartCare User Guide

Welcome to SmartCare. This guide explains how to navigate the platform based on your assigned role.

## 1. For Patients
**Goal**: Find the right doctor and manage your health appointments.
- **Dashboard**: View your upcoming schedule and quick-access your recent doctors.
- **Find Doctors**: Navigate to the "Doctors" tab to search the medical directory by name or specialization.
- **Booking**: Click on a Doctor, select an available date and time slot, and confirm your booking.
- **Profile**: Keep your medical history, blood group, and contact information up-to-date in the Profile tab so your doctors have accurate information before your visit.

## 2. For Doctors
**Goal**: Manage your availability and oversee your patient queue.
- **Dashboard**: Get a quick glance at today's appointments and your overall completion metrics.
- **Manage Slots**: Navigate to the "Slots" tab to open up your schedule. You can create multiple blocks of availability. Note: You cannot delete a slot once a patient has booked it.
- **Appointments**: View your patient queue. Click "Details" to review the patient's medical history and appointment notes. 
- **Status Updates**: Use the dropdown in the Appointment Details page to move an appointment from "Pending" to "Confirmed", and eventually to "Completed".

## 3. For Administrators
**Goal**: Oversee system health, manage staff, and analyze platform usage.
- **Dashboard**: View high-level metrics including total registered users, total doctors, and total appointments.
- **User Management**: Navigate to the "Users" tab. You can search for any user and immediately deactivate their account if necessary.
- **Doctor Oversight**: View the "Doctors" tab to track how many appointments a specific doctor is handling and view their completion rates.
- **Reports**: Access the "Reports" tab to view visual charts outlining the platform's daily and weekly booking trends.
